About Us

Welcome to Passticeria, a family-run business in Jelah since 1981. We take pride in our tradition of serving the best ice cream in town, alongside delicious waffles, coffee, lemonade, and sundaes. However, our main focus has always been on perfecting our ice cream, which remains the heart of our offerings

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to serve the best ice cream and a variety of other treats to our customers, ensuring they leave with a smile. We are committed to providing top-quality products and excellent service, always hoping to make every visit a happy one for our valued guests.

Our Team

We are a group of professionals passionate about delivering excellence.
